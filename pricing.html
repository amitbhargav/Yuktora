FAQ

Common questions

What is the Anthropic API key?
A key from Anthropic (makers of Claude AI). You get ~$5 free credit at console.anthropic.com — enough for 300+ tailors. Free plan: bring your own key. Pro: we handle it.
Is my data secure?
All data is stored only in your browser's localStorage. Nothing goes to our servers. Only Anthropic's API is called for AI features.
What if I clear my browser data?
Use Export JSON in Settings to back up your data. You can reimport on any device. Export weekly during an active search.
Does it auto-apply to jobs?
No — intentionally. Auto-applying risks LinkedIn and Naukri account bans. HireTrackAI does 90% of the work. You click Apply.
Can I use it for international jobs?
Yes. Works with any job description globally — LinkedIn, Wellfound, Glassdoor, company career pages. Auto-detects visa sponsorship.
Can I cancel Pro anytime?
Yes, anytime. No questions asked. Data stays accessible after cancelling. Lifetime has nothing to cancel — it's yours forever.
