Risks & limitations
Job site scraping is not supported
LinkedIn, Naukri, Indeed block browser scraping (CORS + bot detection). Paste the JD manually — this also keeps you within their ToS.
API key stored locally
Your Anthropic key is in browser localStorage. Anyone with dev tools access on your machine can see it. Don't use on shared devices.
Auto-applying is disabled by design
Auto-submissions on LinkedIn/Naukri can get your account banned and often send incomplete applications. You always click Apply yourself.
Your data never leaves your browser
All jobs, applications, and profile data sit in localStorage. Only AI processing calls reach Anthropic's servers.
Cost is minimal
~₹0.25 per AI resume tailor. 10 tailors/day = ₹2.50/day = ₹75/month. Far cheaper than any paid job service.
Data management
All data is stored locally in your browser. Clear browser storage to wipe everything — export first to back up.
